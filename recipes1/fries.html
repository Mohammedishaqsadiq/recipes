French Fries

French fries are thin strips of potatoes that are deep-fried until they become crispy and golden brown. They are a popular and widely consumed snack or side dish in many parts of the world.

Ingredients :

  • Potatoes
  • Cooking oil (vegetable, peanut, or canola)
  • Salt
  • Optional: Black pepper, Paprika, Garlic powder, Onion powder, Cayenne pepper

Directions :

  1. Select and Wash Potatoes: Choose potatoes that are suitable for frying, such as russet potatoes. You can peel them if you prefer, or leave the skin on for a rustic look. Wash the potatoes thoroughly.
  2. Cutting: Cut the potatoes into evenly sized sticks or wedges. You can use a sharp knife or a French fry cutter for uniform shapes. Aim for about 1/4 to 1/2 inch in thickness.
  3. Serve: Serve the French fries immediately while they're still hot and crispy. You can enjoy them on their own or with ketchup, mayonnaise, or your favorite dipping sauce.
